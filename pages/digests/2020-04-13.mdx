# [React Native is the Future of Mobile at Shopify](https://engineering.shopify.com/blogs/engineering/react-native-future-mobile-shopify)

After the somewhat unnecessary discussion into the risks and opportunities of using react native (which border on platitudes, depending on how often you've heard them), the author puts forward clear and concise examples of a how Spotify has gradually, and **thoughtfully** begun its migration. It's a good example of how to go about experimenting with a new technology and using real performance indicators to legitimize the pursuit. Many companies would not have invested in the "horse race" between two competing technologies.

Finally, this story is a nice contrast to the long article from AirBnb in late 2018 about why they are moving away from react native.
