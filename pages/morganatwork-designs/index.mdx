# Everything should be in figma

I firmly believe you have to build your product in figma long before you build it in code. Design it multiple times even, because you can iterate a **helluva** lot faster in figma than you can in code (do I need to prove this?). And you can collaborate more effectively too. 

So here are the designs I drew up for this site, plus my inspiration board. I even took a snapshot of an early iteration of the design just for posterity.
