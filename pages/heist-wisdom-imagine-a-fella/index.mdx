# Imagine a fella

There's a bit of dialogue in the movie [heist](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0252503) that amounts to one of my guiding principles:

<Heist />

Everyone has their own take on the "Imposter Syndrome", and the last thing I want to do is pile on.
But, it's a similar concept the one here. Conceptualize a person who is more skilled, more disciplined, more stoic, and more capable than you, and **do** what that person would do. Act like that person. Pretend.


The distinction, I think, is that it is not a syndrome; it's a practice. 
Imagine someone smarter than you, and do what he (or she) would do. That's how you fix the boat. (Oh yea, it's a boat - that's what Gene Hackman fixed in the movie)
